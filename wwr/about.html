History

Splash White Water Rafting began over 25 years ago with a simple mission: bring unforgettable river adventures to thrill-seekers of all ages. What started with one raft and a passion for the outdoors has grown into a trusted rafting company serving thousands each year.

Our certified guides are trained extensively in river navigation and safety. We continue to expand our routes, offering both relaxing scenic floats and challenging white-knuckle rapid experiences for adventure seekers.
