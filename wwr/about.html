History

Splash White Water Rafting began over 25 years ago with a mission to share the excitement and beauty of river adventures with the world. Starting with a single raft and a passion for the outdoors, the company has grown into a trusted leader in guided rafting tours.

Our certified guides bring years of training and experience to ensure each trip is both exhilarating and safe. Whether you’re new to rafting or an experienced rafter, we offer an unforgettable journey down some of the most stunning rivers.
