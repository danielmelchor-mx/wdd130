Family Adventure (Class I-II)

Perfect for beginners and families with small children. Enjoy a half-day of gentle currents, bird watching, and a picnic on the riverbank. It's a relaxing introduction to the world of rafting.

Duration: Half Day

Minimum Age: 6 years

Extreme Challenge (Class IV+)

For adrenaline seekers only. This full-day trip takes you through the biggest and most challenging rapids in the region. Previous experience and excellent physical condition are required.

Duration: Full Day

Minimum Age: 18 years

3-Day Expedition (Class III)

A total immersion in nature. This package includes camping, gourmet grilled meals, and navigating moderate rapids. Learn basic survival skills and enjoy nights under the stars.

Duration: 3 Days / 2 Nights

Minimum Age: 12 years
